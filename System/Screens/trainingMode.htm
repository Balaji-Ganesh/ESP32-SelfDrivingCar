Training Mode

In this mode, car is set in training mode. For purpose of training - all the navigation controls, camera feed and ultrasonic feed are record for training.

The user will be like a guide in issuing proper controls based on the surrounding environments. Note that, the quality of training data has direct impact on training. i.e., to say - if had bad training, then performs worst, vice versa if good training.
