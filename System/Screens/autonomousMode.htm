Autonomous Mode

In this mode, car runs by itself based on the training given in training mode. User can tweak between various features and parameters to effectively understand the decisions taken by the car.

Choose the autonomous level: Radio buttons
  • Semi-autonomous - Car makes all the decisions based on inputs provided, user has the control to decide navigations.
  • Fully-autonomous - Car makes all the decisions. User will just be like a spectator. No manual intervention.
